AI-related incidents can move faster than traditional playbooks because automation amplifies scope.

## Simulate high-impact scenarios

Model prompt injection, output manipulation, and sensitive data leakage events.

## Include cross-functional decision points

Security, legal, compliance, product, and executive teams need synchronized response rules.

## Measure response performance

Track triage speed, containment quality, and escalation timing for each scenario.

## Feed outcomes into architecture

Incident exercise learnings should directly shape control priorities and policy updates.
