# Brand Voice Guide

## Positioning

Vitruvius Cyber Systems speaks with executive clarity and technical edge. The target tone is a 50/50 blend of enterprise credibility and cyber-futurist confidence.

## Voice Principles

- Lead with decision impact, not feature language.
- Use concise technical terminology tied to business risk outcomes.
- Keep copy assertive and precise.
- Introduce cinematic language sparingly as framing, not hype.

## Approved Tone Examples

- "Harden identity boundaries before automation expands blast radius."
- "Convert AI uncertainty into governed execution pathways."
- "Design security posture that survives adversarial pressure and audit scrutiny."

## Avoid

- Overstated marketing claims.
- Excessive slang or dystopian references.
- Vague promises without implementation guidance.
