# AI Security Executive Brief

## 90-Day Priorities

1. Inventory all active AI use cases, models, and data access paths.
2. Assign executive and technical ownership for AI risk decisions.
3. Define approval gates for model deployment and agent tooling access.
4. Perform threat modeling for prompt injection, data exfiltration, and tool abuse.
5. Enforce non-human identity controls for keys, service accounts, and agents.
6. Build control-to-evidence mapping for your required compliance frameworks.
7. Launch red-team validation for highest-risk AI workflows.
8. Create monthly board-level reporting on AI risk posture and remediation velocity.
